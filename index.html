Frequently asked

Questions are welcome here.

Who is Tellico Together for?

Tellico Together is for residents who care about connection, constructive participation, and the future of their master-planned or active lifestyle community.

Do I need to be a member to participate?

No. Start by reading, attending a gathering, or sending us an idea. We want participation to feel open and approachable.

How can I share an idea or a concern?

Use the Google Form or send us an email. We are listening for questions, feedback, and practical ideas that can help neighbors thrive together.

Is Tellico Together affiliated with Tellico Community Alliance?

Yes. Tellico Together is powered by Tellico Community Alliance, Inc, with a focus on positive community connection and constructive dialogue.

About us

Better together, by design.

Tellico Together helps residents connect across interests and experiences, so more people can take part in the life of their community. We are here to make it easier to learn, listen, share ideas, and turn neighborly energy into meaningful participation.
